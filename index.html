Different Cryptocurrencies

Bitcoin
It is the first decentralized cryptocurrency. Nodes in the peer-to-peer bitcoin network verify transactions through cryptography and record them in a public distributed ledged, AKA a blockchain, without central oversight. Mining achieves consensus between nodes and it secures the bitcoin blockchain. Mining consumes a lot of electricity and is crticized for the environmental impacts from it.
  • Plural: Bitcoins
  • Symbol: ₿
  • Code: BTC
  • Initial Release: 0.1.0 / 9 January 2009
  • Conversion: 3,975,501.44PHP
  • Website: bitcoin.org
Ethereum
Is a decentralized blockchain with smart contract functionality. Ether is the cryptocurrency of the platform. Second to bitcoin in market capitalization.
  • Plural: Ether
  • Symbol: Ξ, ♦
  • Code: ETH
  • Initial Release: 15 July 2015
  • Conversion: 140,952.12PHP
  • Website: ethereum.org
Ripple
Is a real-time gross settlement system, currency exchange and remittlance network open to financial institutions worldwide and made by Ripple Labs Inc.
  • Plural: XRP
  • Symbol: XRP
  • Code: XRP
  • Initial Release: 2012
  • Conversion: 29.52PHP
  • Website: ripple.com
